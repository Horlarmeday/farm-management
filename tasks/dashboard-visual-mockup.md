## Key Visual Elements Illustrated:

### 1. Left Sidebar (280px)
- **Kuyash Farm Management** branding with green leaf logo
- Navigation menu with 12 items including Dashboard, Analytics, Poultry, Livestock, Fishery
- User profile section at bottom with avatar and logout option
- Dark green theme (#059669) with white text and icons

### 2. Main Dashboard Content
- **Professional Header**: Clean title "Dashboard" with subtitle and action buttons
- **KPI Cards Grid**: 4 cards showing Total Animals (1,247), Active Farms (24), Revenue (₦45,200), Alerts (3)
- **Module Cards**: 3 large cards for Poultry, Livestock, and Fishery management with statistics
- **Visual Hierarchy**: Card-based layout with shadows, rounded corners, and hover effects

### 3. Right Widgets Panel
- **Quick Stats**: Revenue, production, and worker statistics
- **Active Alerts**: Color-coded notifications for urgent items
- **Recent Activities**: Timeline of farm operations and updates

### 4. Design Characteristics
- **Color Scheme**: Green primary (#059669), white backgrounds, slate text
- **Typography**: Inter font family with proper hierarchy
- **Spacing**: Consistent 8px grid system
- **Interactions**: Hover effects, smooth transitions, professional animations
- **Responsive**: Mobile-friendly with collapsible sidebar
